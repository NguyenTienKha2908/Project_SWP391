Design Consultation

KIRA Jewelry will review the design samples and provide a quotation. The product price depends on the design and type of gemstone you wish to use. The quotation will be based on the current gold and silver prices.

Drafting the Design

Once the design and product price are agreed upon, customers will transfer funds or place a 50% deposit in person.

Review Your Design

We will create a 3D model of your jewelry design for your review. After approval, VNJ will proceed with crafting the jewelry. The entire customization process typically takes 3 to 7 days.

Completion and Delivery

The product is ready to order!
